# Make offer

- Buyer can offer to the sell post with a offer price . A buyer can send multiple offer at the same time. A seller can get multiple offer from the buyer.

![Make offer](/screenshots/makeoffer.png)

# My offer

- Buyer can see offered post in My offer section.

![My offer](/screenshots/myoffer.png)

# Offer buyer

- Seller can see buyer's offered post in Offer buyer section and seller can accept and reject that particular offer.

![Offer buyer](/screenshots/offerbuyer.png)

# Pay on order after approve order

- when a Seller accept that buyer offer. A buyer can place order that offered post by card or place normal order

![Pay on order after approve order](/screenshots/offerorder.png)
